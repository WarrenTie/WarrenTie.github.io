Weather Stage

Team Project | 1 Month

The project utilizes sensors, motors, mechanical, and artistic elements to craft an interactive, purposeless yet entertaining handcrafted stage, designed purely for fun and engagement.

Interactivity

Using triggers like magnetism, sound, and flames, the project simulates five distinct weather changes and scenarios by employing LEDs, loudspeakers, and motors as output elements.

Process

We utilized laser cutting, 3D printing, and artistic materials to create the casing and components. After building the circuit and coding, soldering and hot glue were used for assembly.

Role

In this duo project, I generated the core idea, managed electronic procurement, implemented interactive functions, built the circuit, and wrote the code, completing tasks efficiently within the limited project timeline.
