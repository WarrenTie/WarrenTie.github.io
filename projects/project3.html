Brickify

Team Project | 5 Months

Brickify is a household waste compression device that compacts paper waste and soft metals like tin foil and cans, reducing waste volume and optimizing space usage.

Background

London's streets face worsening rubbish issues due to public bin removal, causing health, environmental, and social hazards. Research highlights a need for affordable, efficient waste compression solutions to address regulatory confusion, user inconvenience, and worker safety concerns.

Process

We designed uniaxial and biaxial compression mechanisms, selecting uniaxial based on the first round of user feedback. We then developed product features, integrated electronics, and refined branding and aesthetics, completing the final prototype.

Validation

To align further with human-centered design, we conducted a second round of user testing, focusing on smooth operation, electronics functionality, compression efficiency, overall aesthetics, and evaluating the product's impact
