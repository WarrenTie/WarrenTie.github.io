BloomBums

Team Project | 3 Months

BloomBums is a sustainable nappy solution addressing the environmental impact of disposable diapers with a 100% compostable, circular system. The subscription service includes regular delivery and collection, ensuring proper composting and sustainability.

Background

Disposable diapers cause significant environmental issues, including landfill waste, long decomposition times, and resource-intensive production, leading to severe pollution and ecological strain.

System

The system uses biodegradable materials, delivers to users, collects after use, cleans, and composts. The compost supports farming, and reusable components are reintegrated, ensuring sustainability.

Role

In the project, I summarized existing principles on sustainable diapers, designed new regulations, and quantified our solution's environmental impact.
