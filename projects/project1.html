ChessMash

Team Project | 5 Months

ChessMash is a compact, multifunctional kitchen tool for beginners. This smart device combines mashing, squeezing, and molding in one handheld unit.

Process

The project's user-centered design process involves in-depth research, needs analysis, multiple design concepts, iterative prototyping, and engineering analysis, resulting in the final prototype.

Function

A powerful pressing function with button control and interchangeable plates quickly processes potatoes, fruits, and dough to the desired texture, ensuring fast preparation and enhanced cooking efficiency.

Role

As CTO, I oversaw the electro-mechanical system's development and testing, managed coding, and conducted power and load calculations for efficiency and reliability.
